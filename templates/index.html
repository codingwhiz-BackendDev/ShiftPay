Simple process

From invoice to payment
in four steps

Less than 3 minutes to create your first invoice and get paid faster than ever before.

1
Create your invoice
Select a client, add your line items, set a due date, and click send. PayTrack auto-generates a professional invoice number and fills in your bank details.
2
Share the link
Copy the unique public URL and send it anywhere — WhatsApp, email, DM, SMS. Your client opens it in any browser, no login needed, and sees exactly what they owe.
3
Reminders run on autopilot
If your client goes quiet, PayTrack sends polite automatic reminders at 3, 7, and 14 days overdue. You focus on work. The system does the chasing.
4
Confirm and close
Client clicks "I have paid." You get an email alert, verify in your bank app, and click confirm on your dashboard. Invoice closed. Money tracked. Done.
